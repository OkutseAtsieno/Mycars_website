About Us

We are a certified dairy farm located at Kinangop in Kenya
We offer milk products that are nutritious and farm fresh, the farm is growing each day and our greatest desire is to bring to existence the consumption of milk products



MISSION

"To provide high-quality, nutritious dairy products to our customers, sourced from well-cared-for, sustainable farms. We are committed to promoting health and well-being through our fresh and wholesome dairy offerings, fostering strong relationships with our farming partners, and contributing positively to the communities we serve."

VISION

"To be a leading and innovative dairy company, recognized for our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where our dairy products are an essential part of a healthy and balanced lifestyle, and where our practices contribute to a more sustainable and resilient food system. We aspire to be a trusted partner to our farmers, employees, customers, and communities, driven by a passion for quality and a dedication to the well-being of both people and the planet."
